The Wife of Them All
[animation film]



The Wife of Them All (2022) combines science fiction with collaborations with (non)actors and activists to document personal and collective experiences of maintaining queer networks/families in the context of booming fertility markets and intensified border regimes in Europe. Anarchic linking of clay animation, timelapse microscopy and feminist video art traditions. How to visualize technologies of reproduction in relation to all the technologies of non-reproduction?

The film premiered at the 27th LesGaiCineMad Festival and travelled to the 69th International Oberhausen Short Film Festival with support from HAMACA moving image platform.
