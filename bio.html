Rebecca Close works across media art and Science and Technology Studies (STS). Their animation films, installations and writing is concerned with reproduction and imagination, in terms of digital and networked memory; scientific imaging; and social reproduction as a site of possibility.

Their research has focused on how poetry & theory, video/film networks, zines & periodicals, or the Internet interface, are taken up by people to alter epistemes and power relations. They are currently a DOROTHY researcher at the Institute for the History of Science (UAB) in Barcelona working on an MSCA project.
