Rebecca Close (b. London, UK) researches at the intersection of science and technology studies and art. Their work considers how contemporary media, including poetry & theory, bodily gestures & sculptural inscription, video/film networks, zines & periodicals, or the Internet interface, are taken up by people to alter reproductive possibility and life.
