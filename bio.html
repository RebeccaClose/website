What other fantasies,
memory, forms of
connection are possible?

Rebecca Close (b. London, UK) researches at the intersection of Science and Technology Studies and art. Their work considers how ancient and contemporary media, such as words, bodily gestures, sculptural inscription, video networks, maga/zines or the Internet, are taken up by people to alter reproductive possibility and life.
